How it works

Four steps to
seamless communication

Whether you sign or speak, Signslator handles the translation so you can focus on the conversation.

01
Open the app
Launch Signslator and select your mode — sign recognition, text input, or voice. Setup takes seconds.
02
Input your message
Use camera for live sign detection, or type and speak to produce animated sign output for your audience.
03
AI translates instantly
Our on-device model processes input in under 300ms with 98.4% accuracy — no lag, no waiting.
04
Share the result
Copy, speak aloud, or display the animated sign — the other person understands immediately.
